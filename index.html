Hi, I'm Kareena — a product engineer who builds real, working products using the latest engineering technologies. From AI-powered mobile apps to full-stack dashboards, I go beyond wireframes — I design, prototype, and engineer end-to-end product experiences.

My approach blends product thinking with engineering execution: I identify pain points, define user journeys, write PRDs, then build functional prototypes using React, Claude AI APIs, modern CSS, and mobile-first design. I don't just plan products — I ship them.
